Trézor Bridge®®

What is Trézor Bridge®®?

Trézor Bridge®® is an essential communication tool that enables secure interaction between your Trézor device and the Trézor Suite application. It serves as a crucial middleware, ensuring safe and encrypted data transfer between your hardware wallet and your computer.

Security First

Trézor Bridge®® implements state-of-the-art encryption protocols to ensure that all communications between your device and computer remain completely secure and private. It creates an impenetrable tunnel for data transfer, protecting your sensitive information from potential threats.

Regular Updates

We regularly update Trézor Bridge®® to ensure optimal performance and security. These updates include the latest security patches, performance improvements, and compatibility enhancements to keep your device running smoothly and securely.
